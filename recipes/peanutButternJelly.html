Diagonally Cut PB&J

A favorite in my household was the peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Simple to make and rich flavors of sweetness and savory all in one. The simplicity of the thing leaves little margin for error.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Sliced Bread

Steps

  1. Apply peanut butter to one slice of bread
  2. Apply jelly to separate slice of bread
  3. Combine 2 slices of bread with the Peanut Butter contacting the Jelly
  4. OPTIONAL: Cut sandwich diagonally, from corner to corner
  5. Enjoy!
