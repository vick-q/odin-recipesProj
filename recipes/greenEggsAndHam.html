Green Eggs and Ham

Green Eggs and Ham served sunny side up

Inspired from the Dr. Seuss story, of the same name, this dish is a hearty breakfast made silly with the shades of green. Don't be fooled, we aren't preparing food well past its expiration, it'll be fresh as ever.

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • One slice of ham
  • Green food coloring

Steps

  1. Crack two eggs over a pan with heat set to low
  2. Add green food coloring to eggs
  3. Plate eggs once Sunny Side Up
  4. Cook ham on medium heat
  5. Add food coloring to one side of ham
  6. Flip ham to cook evenly
  7. Add food coloring
  8. Plate ham with eggs once fully cooked
  9. Enjoy!
